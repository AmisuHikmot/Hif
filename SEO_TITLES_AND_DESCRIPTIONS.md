## SEO Best Practices Applied

### Title Tags
- ✅ 50-60 characters
- ✅ Brand name included
- ✅ Primary keyword at beginning or end
- ✅ Unique for each page
- ✅ No keyword stuffing

### Meta Descriptions
- ✅ 155-160 characters
- ✅ Calls-to-action included
- ✅ Keyword naturally included
- ✅ Descriptive and compelling
- ✅ Accurate to page content

### Keywords
- ✅ Target keywords selected
- ✅ Long-tail keywords included
- ✅ Geographic location (Nigeria, HIF)
- ✅ Brand variations (HIF, Hamduk)
- ✅ Related search terms

### OpenGraph Tags
- ✅ `og:title` - Page title
- ✅ `og:description` - Social description
- ✅ `og:url` - Full page URL
- ✅ `og:siteName` - "Hamduk Islamic Foundation"
- ✅ `og:image` - Social sharing image

---

## Implementation Notes

1. **Title Format**: Primary Keyword | Secondary Info | Brand Name
2. **Description Format**: Clear value proposition + CTA
3. **Keywords**: Mix of broad terms and long-tail phrases
4. **Branding**: Always use full name in first instance, HIF abbreviation after
5. **URLs**: All use https://www.hif.com.ng (with www)

## Monitoring & Updates

Check monthly:
- Google Search Console for click-through rates
- Average position in search results
- Search appearance for rich results
- Indexing coverage

Update titles/descriptions if:
- Click-through rate drops below 2%
- Page content significantly changes
- Google suggests changes in Search Console
- New keyword opportunities identified
